Monster Survivors

Face the relentless horde, evolve your power, and survive the night!

About the Game

Monster Survivors is an intense, action-packed survival game that throws you into a desperate fight against endless waves of menacing creatures. Trapped in a cursed land, your only goal is to survive as long as possible. Each monster you defeat grants you experience, allowing you to level up and unlock powerful new abilities and devastating weapon upgrades.

Strategy is key. Will you focus on area-of-effect attacks to control the crowd, or enhance your speed and defense to weave through danger? Every choice matters and every run is a new challenge.

How to Play

The controls are simple, but the challenge is immense.

  • Movement: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character.
  • Attacking: Your character attacks automatically. Focus on positioning and dodging!
  • Upgrades: Collect experience gems from fallen enemies to level up. Choose one of three random upgrades each time to build your power.

Key Features

  • Endless Horde Mode: Fight against ever-growing waves of monsters.
  • Evolving Abilities: Hundreds of upgrade combinations for a unique experience every game.
  • Simple Controls, Deep Strategy: Easy to learn, difficult to master.
  • Instant Action: Play directly in your browser with no downloads required.
