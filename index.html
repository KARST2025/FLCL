What Is Monster Survivors?

Monster Survivors is a fast-paced wave survival game where you defeat swarms of enemies, collect power-ups, and stack upgrades to create broken builds. Each run is short, punchy, and rewarding — perfect for quick sessions or deep min-max runs.

Core Loop

Survive waves, earn XP, pick upgrades, repeat. Skill and builds both matter.

Progression

Unlock characters, weapons, and meta perks to push higher difficulties.

Controls

WASD to move, mouse to aim (if applicable). Mobile: on-screen controls.

Performance

Runs smoothly in modern browsers with fast load and low overhead.

How to Play

  1. Use the Play button to load the game instantly.
  2. Move to dodge enemies and collect experience gems.
  3. Pick upgrades that synergize with your current build.
  4. Survive longer waves to unlock stronger perks and characters.
  5. Experiment with different combos to break the meta.
